<p><strong>Investment Analysis:</strong> To understand the potential returns from this property, let's calculate the cap rate:</p>

<p><strong>Cap Rate</strong> = ($270,000 / $5,250,000) x 100 = 5.14%</p>

<p>For a luxury property in a prime location like Las Vegas, a cap rate of 5.14% is appealing. It signifies a stable and lucrative investment, especially when considering the potential for property appreciation in the booming Las Vegas real estate market.</p>
